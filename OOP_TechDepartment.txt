A requirement comes for a company, which they want a software to manage their employees.
They are a software company, they have CTO, Team Leads and Engineers.
Your software should able to let them manage their tech department.

Engineer should have name, id, DoB and base salary.
Team lead should have similar infor like an engineer, 
But also, under a team lead there should be a list of engineers.
CTO has similar info,but under a CTO should be a list of team leads.

At the end of a year, CTO will give bonus to team leads 
and team lead can decide how much they would share to engineers.
Those bonus will add up to base salary.
